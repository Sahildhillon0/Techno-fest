Cultural EVENTS

Solo Dance

Participants showcase their individual dance skills and expressiveness in a solo performance. Dancers may choose various dance styles, from classical to contemporary or cultural forms.

Duet Dance

Participants perform in pairs, showcasing synchronized movements and coordination. Duet dances often emphasize storytelling and harmony between the performers.

Group Dance

Involves coordinated performances by a group of participants, showcasing teamwork and creativity. Group dances may incorporate various styles and themes, highlighting unity and synchronization.

Solo Song

Participants demonstrate their vocal abilities and stage presence through a solo musical performance. Performances may encompass diverse musical genres, showcasing individual expression and talent.

Duet Song

Participants perform a musical piece in pairs, emphasizing harmonization and mutual musicality. Duet songs may cover a wide range of musical genres and emotional themes.

Group Song

Involves a group performance, showcasing vocal harmony and collaborative musical expression. Participants may choose diverse musical genres and themes for their group song performance.

Mimicry

Mehandi

Participants demonstrate their artistic skills in applying traditional henna designs on hands or other body parts. Emphasizes intricate design, cultural artistry, and creativity in crafting henna patterns.

Rangoli

Participants create colorful and intricate designs on the floor using colored powders, grains, or flower petals. Emphasizes creativity, precision, and cultural artistry in crafting decorative patterns.

Best Out of Waste

Participants create innovative and useful items from discarded or repurposed materials, showcasing creativity and resourcefulness. Emphasizes sustainability, creativity, and the ability to transform waste into something valuable.

Collage Making

Participants create artistic compositions by assembling diverse materials such as photographs, paper, and other elements. Emphasizes creativity, visual composition, and storytelling through mixed media.
