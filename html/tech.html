TECHNICAL EVENTS

Technical Quiz

A competition testing participants' technical knowledge through questions and challenges.Covers topics like programming languages, algorithms, and emerging technologies.

Technical Debate

Engages participants in structured arguments and discussions on technology-related topics .Topics may include privacy, AI, cybersecurity, and other current tech issues.

Management Games

Tests and enhances participants' management and decision-making skills. Simulations cover areas like resource allocation, project management, and crisis handling.

On the Spot Coding

Participants solve coding problems within a specified time, showcasing their problem-solving abilities. Challenges may include algorithmic puzzles, debugging tasks, and optimization challenges.

Exhibition of Projects

Provides an opportunity for participants to showcase innovative projects and ideas. Projects may include software applications, hardware prototypes, sustainability initiatives, and social impact solutions.

Logo Designing

Allows participants to showcase their creativity and design skills by creating logos. Emphasizes the ability to communicate a brand's message and identity through visual design.

AD Mad

Challenges participants to create and present entertaining advertisements for products or services. Emphasizes creativity, persuasive communication, and effective storytelling.

Startup Ideas

Encourages participants to present innovative and viable business concepts. Focuses on creativity, market analysis, and the ability to articulate a compelling business idea.

Group Discussion

Engages participants in structured discussions on various topics to assess communication and teamwork skills. Topics may include current affairs, social issues, and ethical dilemmas.

E-Sports

Offers competitive gaming challenges in popular video games. Emphasizes strategic thinking, teamwork, and skill in specific video game genres.

Earning by Learning

Encourages participants to present innovative ideas for earning while learning. Focuses on entrepreneurship, creativity, and practical problem-solving skills.
